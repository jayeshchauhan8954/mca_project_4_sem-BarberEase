## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the BarberEase website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** or **"Register"**
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Account Type:
     - **Customer**: Book appointments
     - **Shop Owner**: Manage barbershops
     - **Staff**: Access as staff member
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll receive a confirmation email
6. Login with your credentials

### Logging In

1. Visit the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## For Customers

### Booking an Appointment

#### Step 1: Browse Shops

1. Go to **Dashboard** or **Shops**
2. Browse available barbershops
3. View shop details:
   - Services offered
   - Staff members
   - Ratings and reviews
   - Business hours

#### Step 2: Select Service

1. Click on your preferred shop
2. Choose from available services:
   - Haircut
   - Beard Trim
   - Shave
   - Hair Styling
   - And more...
3. View service details:
   - Price
   - Duration
   - Description

#### Step 3: Choose Staff & Time

1. Select your preferred staff member (or "Any Available")
2. Pick a date from the calendar
3. Choose an available time slot
4. Add any special requests in the notes

#### Step 4: Make Payment

1. Review booking details
2. Advance payment required (10-25% of service price)
3. Choose payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - UPI
   - Net Banking
   - Wallets
4. Complete secure payment via Razorpay
5. Receive booking confirmation

### Managing Your Bookings

#### View Bookings

3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

#### Reschedule a Booking

1. Go to your booking details
2. Click **"Reschedule"**
3. Select new date and time
4. Confirm changes

## For Shop Owners

### Setting Up Your Shop

#### Creating a Shop Profile

1. Go to **Shops** page
2. Click **"Add Shop"**
3. Fill in shop details:
   - **Shop Name**: Your barbershop name
   - **Address**: Complete address with pincode
   - **Phone**: Shop contact number
   - **Email**: Shop email (optional)
   - **Description**: About your shop
4. Set business hours for each day
5. Configure shop settings:
   - Advance payment percentage (10-25%)
   - Slot duration (15/30/60 minutes)
   - Buffer time between appointments
   - Maximum advance booking days
6. Click **"Create Shop"**

#### Managing Multiple Shops

- View all your shops in one place
- Switch between shops easily
- Each shop has separate:
  - Staff
  - Services
  - Bookings
  - Analytics

### Managing Staff

#### Adding Staff Members

1. Go to **Staff** page
2. Click **"Add Staff"**
3. Enter staff details:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Email (optional)
   - Specialization (e.g., "Hair Stylist")
4. Upload profile photo (optional)
5. Assign services they can provide
6. Set their working hours
7. Click **"Add Staff Member"**

#### Setting Staff Availability

1. Select a staff member
2. Click **"Set Availability"**
3. For each day:
   - Set working hours
   - Add break times
   - Mark days off
4. Save changes

Staff availability is immediately reflected in the booking system.

4. Fill in service details:

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is BarberEase free to use?**  
A: Yes, for customers it's completely free. Shop owners have a free trial, then subscription plans.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to you.

**Q: Can I book for someone else?**  
A: Yes, enter their details during booking or add them as a family member in your profile.

### Booking Questions

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**  
A: Depends on shop settings, typically 1-30 days in advance.

**Q: Can I book without payment?**  
A: No, advance payment (10-25%) is required to confirm bookings and reduce no-shows.

**Q: What if I'm late?**  
A: Please inform the shop. Slots have a 10-minute grace period. After that, it may be marked as no-show.

**Q: Can I book multiple services together?**  
A: Yes, create separate bookings or contact the shop for package deals.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**  
A: Yes, we use Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We don't store card details.

**Q: How do I get a refund?**  
A: Refunds are processed automatically to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

**Q: Can I pay cash instead?**  
A: Online bookings require advance payment. Cash payment is available for walk-ins.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Credit/Debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular wallets via Razorpay.

### Shop Owner Questions

**Q: How much does it cost for shop owners?**  
A: Free for first month, then ‚Çπ999/month per shop.

**Q: Can I manage multiple shops?**  
A: Yes, you can manage unlimited shops from one account.

**Q: How do I add staff?**  
A: Go to Staff page ‚Üí Add Staff ‚Üí Fill details ‚Üí Save.

**Q: Can staff update their own schedule?**  
A: Yes, staff can update their availability after you grant them access.

## Glossary

- **Advance Payment**: Partial payment (10-25%) to confirm booking
- **Buffer Time**: Gap between appointments for setup/cleanup
- **No-Show**: Customer didn't arrive for appointment
- **Slot**: Available time for booking
- **Walk-in**: Customer without prior booking
